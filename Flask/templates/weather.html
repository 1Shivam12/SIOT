Weather API: Open Weather Maps

The data streamed from the weather API collected data over a period of 4 days. The main parameters that were of interest were

  1. Temperature (°c)
  2. Humidity (%)
  3. Pressure (Bar)
It was found that the pressure was essentially constant throughout the data collection period and hence this was not controlled in the study.

The data was normalized and plotted against time to see its variation across the data collection period. The graph confirms there is no correlation between the 2 data sets.
